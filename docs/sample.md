# Northwind Robotics — Company Handbook (Sample)

## About the Company

Northwind Robotics is a mid-sized robotics company founded in 2011 by
**Dr. Mara Voss** and **Idris Kane**. The company is headquartered in
**Tallinn, Estonia**, with a secondary engineering office in **Lisbon,
Portugal**. Northwind designs autonomous warehouse robots for logistics
operators across Europe.

## Products

Northwind's flagship product is the **Otter-9** autonomous mobile robot. The
Otter-9 navigates warehouse aisles using a sensor stack that combines LiDAR,
stereo cameras, and wheel odometry. The robot's planning software, **PathWeaver**,
runs on an in-house real-time operating system called **NorthOS**.

A second product line, the **Otter-Lift**, extends the Otter-9 platform with a
scissor-lift module for retrieving items from high shelves. The Otter-Lift
shares the same NorthOS firmware and PathWeaver planner as the Otter-9.

## People

Dr. Mara Voss serves as Chief Executive Officer and leads long-term research.
Idris Kane is the Chief Technology Officer and owns the NorthOS roadmap. The
PathWeaver team is led by **Sofia Reyes**, who reports to Idris Kane. Sofia
previously worked at a mapping startup acquired by Northwind in 2017.

## Partnerships

Northwind Robotics partners with **Baltic Freight**, a logistics operator that
runs the largest fleet of Otter-9 units. Baltic Freight is headquartered in
**Riga, Latvia**. Northwind also collaborates with **Lisbon Technical
University** on a research grant focused on multi-robot coordination.

## Support and SLAs

Customers on the Enterprise plan receive 24/7 support with a four-hour response
SLA. Firmware updates to NorthOS are delivered over the air on a monthly cadence.
The Otter-Lift requires an additional annual safety inspection because of its
lifting mechanism.
